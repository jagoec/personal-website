# First Garden Update

**March 15, 2024**

This is my first garden update for 2026. I'm excited to pick up where I left off from last year. The theme of this year is: "Try it!"

## What I've Done

- Added 6 ft<sup>3</sup> dirt to the garden bed to get it closer to the height of the walls
- Bought seeds: 
    - Flowers: Zinnia (2x), Sunflower (Skyscraper and Teddy Bear), Marigolds, Echinacea (realized they are perennials so see you next year)
    - Herbs: Basil, Cilantro, Parsley, Dill
    - Vegetables: Peas, Lettuce, Scallions, Cucumbers

- Created a cover for the bed out of old wire panels that look like they were from a rabbit hutch.
- Planted peas, lettuce, and onions, both outside and in the peat pellets under the grow lights.
